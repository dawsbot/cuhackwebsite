Prepare for Spring 2015. Something big is about to happen.

  • What is CUHack?
  • CUHack is the University of Colorado's first official national hackathon. Over 200 hackers from across the nation will arrive in Boulder to represent their universities at the only event of its kind in all of the Mountain timezone. When you throw engineers, designers, programmers, and big cash prizes into one event it has to be memorable.
